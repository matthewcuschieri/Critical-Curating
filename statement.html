The Arecibo Message was the first radio signal sent by humanity sent to space in hopes of finding alien life. The broadcast, sent into a major star cluster, has yet to glean an answer. In ‘Arecibo: Missed Connections’ we examine what exactly a missed connection is, and what it entails to seek and not be responded to. These may be unanswered relationships with the world, or with strangers, or with our pasts. We evaluate what makes these mundane, open-ended realities ‘alien’.” Further, we hope to bring forth, through the works curated, the points of absence that emerge when these connections are lost — these gaps thus become the origins of creativity.
